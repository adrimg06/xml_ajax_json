Simple XML Questions

2. Please show (in XML syntax) the hierarchy of the element book with the subelements chapters and author. author has the further subelements name and address.

<book>
  <chapters>
    <!-- chapter content here -->
  </chapters>
  <author>
    <name>Author Name</name>
    <address>Author Address</address>
  </author>
</book>
